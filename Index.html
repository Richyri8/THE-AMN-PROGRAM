What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a business model where you earn a commission by promoting products or services from other companies. As an affiliate, you partner with a business, share unique referral links, and get rewarded for driving sales, leads, or other desired actions. This allows you to earn money without creating your own product or service. It is a flexible, performance-based way to earn, meaning you get paid based on results, making it scalable and beginner-friendly.
